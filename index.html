Our Location

Moore B&B is perfectly located in the heart of Galway City Center. Located along the Famous Long Walk, our location provides exceptional veiws of Galway Bay. We are only a one minute walk from the historic Spanish Arch, and 5 minutes from the central Eyre Square.

Galway is easily accessible from anywhere in Ireland. Serviced by excellent bus and train routes, well connected roads, and two airports, we are excited to welcome you from anywhere in the world!

Moore B&B is a 10 minute walk from the main bus and rail stations in the city center. Additionally, there is a local bus stop only 50 yards from the B&B.

The B&B is 80 Km (40 miles) from Shannon airport. We are only a 2 hour drive from Dublin airport. Free private parking available. Taxis available on request.
