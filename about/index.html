About Moore B&B

Our History

Moore B&B has been proudly owned and run by the Moore family for the past 20 years.

Located on the historic and famously picturesque Long Walk, Moore B&B resides in a building construced in the 1800's. The inside of the B&B has been expertly desgined and decorated, to preserve the history of the building while balancing a stunningly modern interior. With beautiful views over Galway Bay and a perfectly central location, everything you could ask for is within walking distance.

Renowned for its comfort, luxury and traditional Irish hospitality, Gillian Moore and family are delighted to welcome you to their home in the heart of Galway.

Our Rooms

Each of our 10 guest rooms has been expertly designed with your comfort in mind. Each room boasts spectacular views over Galway Bay, and all rooms are en-suite to ensure privacy and comfort. All of our rooms are non-smoking.

We have a wide range of room types to cater to all our guests. Leisure or business, family or friends, individuals or groups we look forward to having you stay with us.
